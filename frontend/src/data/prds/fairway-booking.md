# Fairway Booking - Product Overview

## Executive Summary

Fairway Booking automates the complex coordination required for group golf bookings, solving the "when and where" problem that prevents many golf groups from playing together. The platform intelligently finds mutual availability across group members and automatically books tee times at preferred courses.

## Market Opportunity

Group coordination is a persistent pain point in recreational golf, with many potential rounds lost due to scheduling friction. The golf booking market is large and growing, with significant opportunity for automation that reduces coordination overhead.

## Product Vision

Eliminate the friction of group golf coordination by automating availability matching and booking processes, enabling more rounds played and stronger group engagement.

## Key Features

- **Intelligent Scheduling**: Automatically finds mutual free time across group members
- **Automated Booking**: Seamless integration with golf course booking systems
- **Preference Learning**: Remembers favorite courses and preferred times
- **Group Management**: Easy coordination for recurring golf groups

## Target Audience

- Regular golf groups (4+ players) who struggle with coordination
- Golf clubs and communities seeking to increase member engagement
- Golfers who want to play more but face scheduling barriers

## Business Model

Transaction-based model with per-booking fees. Potential revenue sharing with golf courses for increased bookings. Premium tier for advanced scheduling features.

## Competitive Advantage

- Focus on group coordination (not just individual booking) addresses unmet need
- Automated matching reduces manual coordination time by 80%+
- High booking success rate (72%) demonstrates product effectiveness

## Traction

Strong early metrics with 72% booking success rate, indicating effective automation and user satisfaction. Growing user base of active golf groups.

## Next Steps

Expanding course partnerships and developing advanced scheduling algorithms to improve success rates further.
